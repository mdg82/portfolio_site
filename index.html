About Me

Hi, I'm Melanie Green! Let's just say me and the outlaw Jesse James would have been practically neighbors here in Kearney, MO where I reside. I have always considered myself technically creative, which is why I have always liked the computer and camera. My first interest was photography and have always loved taking nature photos, and got interested in sports photography when i worked as a sports newspaper photographer after college. I strayed away from photography and tried my hand at banking, but it did not suit me, and wanted to return to my passion of being technically creative. Nowadays, I am in pursuit of becoming a front-end web developer, but willing to take and use my own photos to include in my website projects whenever possible. I also like to work on retouching old photographs in my spare time.
